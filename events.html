Rally for Bally

Saturday 6th June 2015 - Celebrity Tennis Charity Event

Buy your tickets here http://www.jumblebee.co.uk/rally4bally

Western Tennis Club are delighted to announce they are organising another Celebrity Tennis Event on 6th June 2015. This year we will again be raising money for The Elena Baltacha Tennis Foundation http://www.ebatuk.org/elena-baltacha-foundation/ and also for Playlist for Life http://www.playlistforlife.org.uk. We will also be making a donation towards research into the rare form of dementia that is affecting well known Scottish tennis player, Malcolm Watt, who has defied doctors'expectations by continuing to play since diagnosis. Malcolm will also participate in the event as one of our "pros".

We are also very excited that Judy Murray and Sally Magnusson will be in attendance on the day to talk about the respective charities. Judy is a patron of EBAT and Sally is co-founder of Playlist for Life.


The format of the day is as follows:

2 pm: doors open

230 - 430 pm: FREE tennis coaching for kids and adults, speed of serve competition and beat the pro! Plus plenty of time to get your raffle tickets. Food and drink will also be on sale.

5 - 6 pm: Take your seats for our Celebrity Tennis Event starring Greg Hemphill, Julie Hemphill, Alan Rough, Sanjeev Kohli, Kaye Adams, Judith Ralston, Alison Walker, Ian McCall and Paul English

6 - 7 pm: Auction and raffle. Judy Murray and Sally Magnusson will speak.

7pm onwards: Stay on and enjoy some tunes with us courtesy of Andy Lowndes. What's on your Playlist for life?


Last year, we raised over £10,000 for our nominated good causes. Please join us in trying to double that amount this year!

On a final note, if you would like to help, please email juliegtennis@gmail.com. We need help on the day but also in the lead up to organise prizes, raffles, auctions, social media, ticketing etc etc. It would be great to hear from you even if you only have a couple of hours to spare. We are also looking for event sponsors to cover costs like hire of the stand.


** All updates regarding this special event will be posted on this page. In addiiton, we have a decicated Facebook page also containing the same information https://www.facebook.com/rally4ballyWesternGlasgow.
