Western Squash


Squash is the game you can play in all weathers, a great bonus for anyone interested in racquet sports in Glasgow! The club has one of the biggest and most active squash sections in Glasgow, catering for all levels of player from complete beginners to juniors and seniors with international experience.

New players are always welcome. Squash facilities and weekly events at the Western Club include:

  • Three heated squash courts
  • 45 minute sessions available from 9.00am until 10.30pm
  • Social squash, 7.00pm - 9.00pm, all year round
  • 6 men's teams, 2 ladies' teams competing in West of Scotland leagues National League team
  • Mini-leagues
  • Open Nights

Interested in joining?

Come along any Sunday evening between 7 and 9.00pm for a free trial session as part of our open night where you can meet Martin Woods, our squash coach, who will be able to provide details about all the club has to offer.

For any other information about squash at the Western Health & Racquets Club, contact the Squash Convenor Geoff Cordwell.

Open-Nights & Mini-leagues

The Open-Nights and Mini-Leagues are open to players of ALL standards. They are great ways to meet new opponents and to improve your game. The turn up and play Open-Nights take place every Sunday from 7.00 to 9.00pm. The Mini-Leagues each consist of 5 players and are graded according to standard with promotion and relegation every 5 to 6 weeks.

Junior Coaching

Junior coaching sessions will be run by our club coach, Martin Woods. The sessions are designed to suit players of all ability and experience. The sessions are scheduled for:

To register your interest, or for further information, contact Martin Woods.

Squash Teams

The Club runs:

  • Six Men's and 2 Ladies' squash teams in the West of Scotland Squash Leagues
  • Matches take place weekly throughout the winter from October until March
  • The West of Scotland Squash Association provides information about current league standings, player rankings and other local squash information

Western's squash ladder can be found at http://ropeyladder.com

Tournaments

  • Club Championships: The Club Championships are held in spring each year. They are open to all players with competitions for men, ladies, junior boys and girls.
