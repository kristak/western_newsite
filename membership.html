Membership

We have several types of Gym only and Gym/Squash/Tennis memberships available to new members.

  • Individual Senior: adults aged 18+ years
  • Couple: two cohabiting adults aged 18+ years
  • Golden Individual: adult aged 60+ years
  • Golden Couple: two cohabiting adults aged 60+ years
  • Family: up to two adults and two children
  • Student: full-time student aged 18+ years (student ID required)
  • Junior: ages 14-17 (gym membership), ages 0-8 and 9-17 (tennis/squash)
  • Off Peak: (gym only) Monday to Friday 7am-5pm, Saturday & Sunday after noon
  • Country: members living more than 30 miles from the club

If you want the fun without the sweat, you can join as a Social member. You'll enjoy a bar open six nights a week and all the social activities of a busy sports club

Want to know more?

If you're still undecided, here are four easy ways to find out more. If you like what you see on your visit, you can join on the spot!

  • Drop in anytime during one of our open-door look-around sessions, Thursday 6.30-8.30pm or Sunday 5.00-7.00pm. Our Head Coach / Club Manager, Ian Campbell, or a member of our staff will be on hand to show you around.
  • Phone us on 0141 339 0065. We can answer your questions, or arrange to show you around at a time that suits you.
  • Send us an email, info@glasgowwestern.co.uk.
  • Sample a coaching and playing session. Just show up any Sunday evening at 5.00pm (tennis) or 7.00pm (squash). There is no obligation. We'll even lend you a racquet.
