Emma Steenson

Chartered Physiotherapist HPC CSP OCPPP

Emma is a chartered physiotherapist with 13 years clinical experience mainly in musculoskeletal and sporting injuries. Having qualified from the University of Ulster, Belfast Emma has worked mainly in private practice within the UK, Ireland, New Zealand and Australia. She is extremely passionate about physiotherapy and has spent her career developing her skills through continued professional development and post graduate training.

She adopts a distinctive 'hands on approach' using a combination of manual therapy, massage, myofascial release and acupunture in her treatments. She is very passionate about exercise and truly believes in the importance of empowering and educating her patients about their injuries. She teaches physio led Pilates classes in the West End which is highly beneficial for alleviating back and neck pain, improving posture and strengthening core stability. Emma has a particular interest in treating ante and post natal mothers presenting with musculoskeletal injuries.

As a busy Mum of two Emma loves exercising herself and enjoys running and cycling. To make an appointment or to find out more information please phone Emma on 07919974271 or email physioform@hotmail.co.uk.

Scott MacAulay

Glasgow City Physiotherapy

Ideally located in the City Centre of Glasgow. Based within fit body training camp, we enjoy close proximity to Glasgow Central station. We have a unique approach to Physiotherapy as we look to treat your injury, the cause of the injury and provide injury prevention advice or management.

We treat a wide range of injuries. Some of these include sports injuries, back/neck pain, headaches, muscle sprains, work related injuries, rehabilitation from surgeries, Osteoarthritis, Childhood and adolescent musculoskeletal disorders among many more. We achieve this by providing various top quality services such as physiotherapy, sports massage, acupuncture, rehabilitation, employ individualized injury prevention strategies and provide strength + Conditioning programmes to patients. We also run a stretching and core class to ensure patients are getting the best out of their treatment. Our terrific facilities aid your rehabilitation and allow you to make the best recovery or simply enjoy your treatment.
