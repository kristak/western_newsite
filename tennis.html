Tennis

We have 6 all-weather tennis courts and an unrivalled range of programmes where you will have the chance to meet and play with lots of people of your own standard.

Tennis Information

For information about tennis at the Western Health & Racquets Club, contact the Tennis Convenor, Ian Campbell











Coaching

Whether you are a beginner or just want to improve we have a large coaching programme.

  • Private or group lessons
  • Weekly team training over the winter for players of team standard
  • Group lessons and team training organised by PCA Master coach Ian Campbell
  • For details of all adult coaching whether for individual or group lessons of any standard: ian@glasgowwestern.co.uk

Inter-club Team Tennis

We have a wide range of teams playing in inter-club leagues.

  • Ten senior teams in the West of Scoland Tennis Leagues (5 Mens & 5 Ladies)
  • Compete in tournaments for singles players, veteran teams, winter league teams and junior teams.

Social Tennis & Mini-Leagues

Social Tennis

  • Thursday 6:30 - 8:30pm
  • Sunday 5:00 - 7:00pm
  • Fun doubles tournaments

Mini-Leagues

Competitive singles mini-leagues are organised each month with approximately 5 players in each league.

Junior Program

We run a popular Junior tennis programme catering for children from 4 years old and upwards that includes the following:

  • Group lessons after school and at weekends during term time
  • Summer and Holiday Camps
  • Schools Project 2014: A Coach, A Court and a Competition sponsored by Clyde Property

For information on any aspect of Junior tennis, visit our Western Tennis coaching blog

Enquiries to: minitennis@glasgowwestern.co.uk
